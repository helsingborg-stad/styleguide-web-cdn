Large search form

The search form basiclly is a input-group (find docs under Forms/Input group).

Example

<form class="search" method="get" action="/">
   <label for="search-keyword" class="sr-only">Sök på Helsingborg.se</label>
   <div class="input-group input-group-lg">
       <input id="search-keyword" autocomplete="off" class="form-control form-control-lg" type="search" name="s" placeholder="Sök på Helsingborg.se">
       <span class="input-group-addon-btn">
           <input type="submit" class="btn btn-primary btn-lg" value="Sök">
       </span>
   </div>
</form>
